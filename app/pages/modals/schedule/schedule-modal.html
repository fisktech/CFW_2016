Schedule Help

How to use the schedule:

The schedule page contains a list of all the events that are taking place during Cyclone Family Weekend. To learn more about an event, simply tap on the event and you can view the event details. To return to the full schedule just cick the back button in the upper left corner.

Each event has a colorered bar on the left side. Events with a green bar are free to the public and anyone can attend. The events in red require tickets that were purchased as part of the registration process. These events have a seating capacity and/or require preparation (chairs, food, etc.).
